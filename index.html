01 — The Opportunity

A Legacy Property Deserving a Global Stage

Thanda Safari presents one of Southern Africa's most compelling ultra-luxury real estate opportunities. The Royal Thanda Club offers private game reserve residences that blend wilderness immersion with world-class design, positioned for a discerning international audience.

This action plan outlines a comprehensive sales and marketing strategy to position the Royal Thanda Club Residences on the global stage, leveraging the editorial authority, network reach, and brand credibility of Forbes Global Properties.

Our approach combines heritage-driven storytelling, cinematic content production, strategic international distribution, and an elite press event to generate qualified buyer engagement, site visits, and concluded transactions within a defined campaign window.

06 — Sales Process

From Enquiry to Completion

A structured, white-glove sales process built around the ACR methodology: Acquisition, Conversion, Retention.

Acquisition

Generate qualified inbound enquiries through Forbes editorial listings, heritage film distribution, targeted social campaigns, and international PR. Every touchpoint drives to a dedicated landing experience.

Discovery Call

Structured qualification call to assess buyer motivation, investment capacity, and timeline. Tailored to UHNW expectations with a consultative, non-transactional approach.

Private Presentation

Bespoke presentation of the Royal Thanda Club, delivered digitally or in person, with full property documentation, lifestyle narrative, and investment overview.

Site Visit

Curated on-site experience at Thanda Safari, including private game drive, residence tour, chef-prepared dinner, and exclusive access to the reserve.

Conversion & Close

Legal, financial, and administrative coordination through to signed offer and transfer. Supported by our local legal partners and the Thanda Group commercial team.

07 — Target Markets

Strategic Global Reach

Five priority markets identified by buyer propensity, travel accessibility, and existing Forbes Global Properties network density.

Middle East

UAE, Saudi Arabia, Qatar. Safari tourism affinity, established South Africa travel corridors, and significant UHNW concentration.

United States

New York, Miami, Los Angeles, San Francisco. North American buyers with appetite for international trophy assets and lifestyle diversification.

Switzerland & German Europe

Zürich, Geneva, Munich, Frankfurt. Privacy-conscious UHNW buyers with strong affinity for conservation-led luxury.

United Kingdom

London and the Home Counties. Historical ties to Southern Africa, established safari culture, and a mature luxury real estate appetite.

Scandinavia

Stockholm, Oslo, Copenhagen. Growing safari and conservation investment interest, with strong purchasing power and global mobility.
