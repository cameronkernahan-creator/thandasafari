Considerations

Based on our initial conversation,
here is what we understand so far.

We have documented what we believe to be the key considerations. We would love for you to tell us if we have this right, and where we may be missing something.

Previous Vendor Performance

Previous international broker relationships have produced limited traction. There is an opportunity to understand what worked and what did not, so we avoid repeating the same approach.

Reaching the Right Buyer

The international buyer appreciates the value of South African nature and lifestyle at a level the domestic market does not always match. Reaching them requires a platform with genuine global credibility.

Brand Standards

Thanda has been deliberately low-profile. Any marketing approach needs to reflect the premium nature of the product and protect the brand's reputation for exclusivity and discretion.
