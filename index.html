Why We Are Here

Thanda deserves a global stage.

Thanda Safari Private Game Reserve stands on the ancestral lands of KwaZulu-Natal, one of the most historically and culturally significant territories in Africa. This is Zululand, home to the great Zulu Kingdom, a civilisation renowned for its warrior heritage, fierce pride, and profound connection to the land. The name Thanda means "Love" in Zulu, a deliberate choice that speaks to the founding philosophy: not conquest, but reverence. The vision is not merely a luxury development. It is a family legacy estate set within the living culture of Zululand, where 16,500 hectares of Big Five wilderness, conservation commitments, and intergenerational memories are woven together into something the market has never seen before.

With 44 full-title freehold villas, 37 still available, and a member profile that already includes European billionaires and global entrepreneurs, Thanda has built extraordinary proof of concept. What it now needs is the international reach to match the scale of the vision. That is precisely what South Africa | Forbes Global Properties exists to deliver.
