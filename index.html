01
The Opportunity

Thanda Safari is one of the most exceptional private safari estates in Africa. The Royal Thanda Club is a once-in-a-generation real estate and lifestyle proposition: 44 exclusive membership positions within a 386-hectare high-security estate inside a 16,500-hectare Big Five game reserve in northern KwaZulu-Natal.

Seven Royal Residences have been completed and sold, entirely off-market and without formal marketing. The remaining positions in Phase 1 and Phase 2 are now being brought to market for the first time through Forbes Global Properties. Phase 3 represents a future launch and is not part of the current campaign.
