The Opportunity

Thanda Royal Residence is one of the most exceptional private safari estates in Africa. The Royal Thanda Club is a once-in-a-generation real estate and lifestyle proposition: exclusive residences within a 386-hectare high-security estate inside a 16,500-hectare Big Five game reserve in northern KwaZulu-Natal.

Seven of twenty-three Royal Residences have already been sold—entirely off-market and without any formal marketing. The remaining 16 positions across Phase 1 and Phase 2 are now being brought to market for the first time through Forbes Global Properties. A future Phase 3 exists but is not part of this campaign.
