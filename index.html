The Opportunity
What we are selling and why the world is ready for it

Thanda Safari is one of the most exceptional private safari estates in Africa. The Royal Thanda Club represents a once-in-a-generation real estate and lifestyle proposition: 44 exclusive membership positions within a 386-hectare high-security estate inside a 16,500-hectare Big Five game reserve in northern KwaZulu-Natal.

Seven Royal Residences have been sold quietly over 24 years with no formal marketing. Sixteen remain available in Phases 1 and 2, with 22 additional sites in Phase 3. The product has spoken for itself. Now, for the first time, we are bringing it to the global stage through Forbes.

Editorial & Content Strategy
The story-first approach confirmed with Forbes editorial team
FORBES.COM EDITORIAL FEATURE

A dedicated story-driven article on Forbes.com focused on Thanda as the editorial centrepiece. Forbes requires pricing to be disclosed (POA not permitted on Forbes.com). The editorial team will interview Pierre directly, craft the narrative, and publish independently under the Forbes contributor code of conduct. No client preview or approval before publication.

FORBES GLOBAL PROPERTIES LISTING

Full property listing on ForbesGlobalProperties.com with imagery, pricing, and property details. Supported by the lifestyle magazine "Story, Life and Style." POA is acceptable on this platform. Same content cannot appear on both platforms (SEO protection).

THE HERITAGE FILM

A cinematic heritage film of approximately 3 to 5 minutes. The editorial team develops the narrative first, and the film is built from that script. The voice of the film will be Germain, Reserve General Manager for 22 years. Tone: cinematic, documentary, emotionally anchored. No corporate voice. No sales language. The film ends with a question: What will you leave behind?

Cameron Kernahan will film at Thanda end of April following editorial narrative delivery. The film is the single most important marketing asset Thanda has been missing. Its purpose is to attract emotional interest, not to share all information, prompting qualified inquiries.

PRESS RELEASE

Crafted by Rosenstein PR in New York (Elaine's involvement). Separate from the Forbes editorial. Timed to coincide with the campaign launch in May.

05
Sales Process
End-to-end from prospect identification to concluded deal
PROSPECTING & LEAD GENERATION
  • Forbes.com editorial and ForbesGlobalProperties.com listing generate inbound inquiries
  • Heritage film distributed across Forbes platforms, social channels, and private screener for qualified prospects
  • Targeted digital campaign through The Miller agency reaching UHNW individuals with interest in South Africa
  • James Edition international luxury marketplace listing
  • Direct outreach to Forbes Global Properties network members across the US, Europe, and Middle East
  • Rosenstein PR press release generating earned media coverage
  • Personal network activation across target markets
QUALIFICATION & ENGAGEMENT
  • Inbound inquiries qualified by Keegan Steyn as Principal Broker
  • One-on-one meetings with qualified prospects (presentation, Q&A, due diligence handover)
  • Follow-up meetings with Pierre Delvaux as required for high-value prospects
  • Comprehensive Buyers Guide and Investment Terms documentation provided
SITE VISIT & CONVERSION
  • Reservation Agreement signed before site visit (builds urgency, commits prospect to a plot and model)
  • Site visit fee paid (offset against purchase price if proceeding, VIP safari if not)
  • Three-night VIP site visit for two at Thanda Safari Private Game Reserve
  • Confirmation of sale during or after site visit
  • Introduction to RSM International for independent tax, legal, and structuring advice
  • Sale Agreement, Building Contract, and related documentation signed
  • Deposit into trust account of Cox Yeats, transferring attorneys
  • Construction period: 12 to 15 months from plot transfer
